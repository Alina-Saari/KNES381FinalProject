Project Details / Background
I was able to download my activity and sleep data off of my FitBit from the last 30 day. I then took that data and created scatterplots for steps vs calories burned and steps vs distance travelled. I also created histograms for steps per day, minutes of REM sleep, minutes of light sleep and minutes of deep sleep.
I also wanted to include a calculation so I decided to calculate basil metabolic rate (BMR) and active metabolic rate (AMR). For BMR I used IF statements to differentiate between the equations for males and females and for AMR I used IF statements for the different levels of activity.
